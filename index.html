My present research focuses on enhancing Large Language Models (LLMs) to achieve excellence in Software Engineering. This entails a dual emphasis on elevating performance and ensuring trustworthiness. Broadly speaking, my work resides at the intersection of machine learning and software engineering. Here, we harness the power of machine learning to alleviate the burdens faced by software developers, ultimately fostering increased productivity. I am privileged to receive guidance from Prof. Prem Devanbu. My works have been published in esteemed Software Engineering venues including ICSE, ESEC/FSE, ASE, TSE, and EMSE.
